Symptoms that affect the whole body or mind

Skin

Includes eczema, impetigo and warts.

Bones

Includes breaks, pains and swellings.

Whole body

Includes high temperature, shivers and tiredness.

Mind

Includes low mood, suicidal thoughts and anxiety.

Symptoms by body part

Head, hair and face

Includes dry eyes, feeling dizzy and lice.

Neck and throat

Includes lumps, sore throat and stiff neck.

Shoulders and arms

Includes loss of feeling, pins and needles and problems carrying things.

Wrists, hands and fingers

Includes grip problems, lack of movement and tense muscles.

Chest and upper back

Includes chest pain, shortness of breath and stiffness.

Stomach and sides

Includes feeling sick, strains and tummy upsets.

Groin and genitals

Includes erection problems, lumps and burning sensations.

Bottom

Includes bleeding, itchines and
soreness.

Hips, legs and feet

Includes aching muscles, pain under the foot and stiff joints.

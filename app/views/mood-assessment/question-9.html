Have you had an anxiety attack (suddenly feeling fear or panic)?

What is an anxiety attack?

An anxiety or panic attack is when you  get a rush of intense mental and  physical symptoms. You may feel an overwhelming sense of  fear. You may also have physical symptoms such as:

  • a racing heartbeat
  • feeling sick
  • sweating
  • trembling or shaking

You may start to avoid certain situations  that most people can cope with because  you fear they’ll trigger another attack.   Most panic attacks last between 5 and 20  minutes. How often you have them will  depend on how severe your condition is.

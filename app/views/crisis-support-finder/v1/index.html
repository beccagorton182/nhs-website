Version 1

Mental health crisis support finder

New crisis support service finder for people who are experiencing a mental health crisis

Users tested with

4

Team members involved

7

How might we questions

  • How might we frame the benefits of the service?
  • How might we make the journey as short as possible?
  • How might we allow users to find their local helpline?
  • How might we not alienate users who don't feel comfortable making phone calls?

Design challenges

  • Balancing trust with ease of use
  • How much information we display about the result

Hypotheses

  • We believe that people in a crisis will be able to enter their postcode into a search box
  • We believe that people experiencing a crisis will click on the number given to them

Prototype journeys

Journeys
  • Version 1 - From Find a Service
  • Version 1 - From Urgent care for mental health page

Main outcomes

  • Users needed to trust the service more, there wasn't enough explanation about why we needed their location
  • Users were not reading the start now page so were unsure what the results page was for
  • The word crisis was stressful and user-led lingo should be used in the title
  • Feedback was overwhelmingly positive about the existence of the services and finder

Coronavirus: update on getting medical help

To protect yourself and others from coronavirus (COVID-19), try to avoid face-to-face medical help if possible.

  • For help from a GP: visit their website, contact them online or phone the surgery.
  • For urgent medical help: visit 111.nhs.uk - call 111 if you need help for a child under 5 or you cannot get help online.
  • For life threatening emergencies: call 999 for an ambulance.

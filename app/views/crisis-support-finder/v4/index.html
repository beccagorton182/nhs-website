Version 4

What's changed?

We created some experimental designs to test reaction to capacity status/waiting times and a new page if we had more information for outside of England users.

Second release was decided and we looked to test displaying more than one result, re-introducing use my location and increasing trust in why we are asking for location.

We added new H1s that adhere closer to the service manual guidance.

Users tested with


14

Team members involved

10

Users with access needs

3

How might we questions

  • How might we better manage user expectations?
  • How might we include results for users outside of England?
  • How might we address and support parents and carers?
  • How might we reassure users to trust our service?
  • How might we display more than one result to users?
  • How might we join up other journeys into the finder?

Design challenges

  • Hint text is being missed but this information is too important for expanders
  • Making two results work and making it clear they're separate services
  • Where to put extra text that might be really reasssuring for people but not overload a page with information

Hypotheses

  • We believe users outside of England will be able to see which helpline is relevant for them in a list
  • We believe putting capacity information below the phone number will not deter users from making a call, but might make them reconsider if they notice it
  • We believe addressing parents and carers directly about accessing young people only services, they will know that they can access them on behalf of a young person
  • We believe listing more than one helpline will confuse or overwhelm users because their cognitive load would be reduced in a crisis
  • We believe putting privacy concerns in expanders will mean users know where to find their answers
  • We believe if we shorten H1 questions, users will feel triggered by these and are less likely to trust the service
  • We believe users do not trust the use my location function

Main outcomes

  • Users would find it useful to have individual pages for outside of England results
  • Users would still call the number even if it was busy - unless it was urgent
  • Users thought it was helpful to have alternative services displayed if the line was busy
  • Users were either overwhelmed or confused by the two results, and were not sure which one they needed to use
  • Users were suspicious or not trusting of the use my location button
  • Some users were uncertain about the new H1s, often putting their own ages in when the task was for somebody else
  • Users found the panel design clearer, but not when there was more than one result
